The ICFAI Tech school presents challenges that blend technology with creativity, including AI-enhanced board games, open mic sessions, photography hunts, data analysis contests, and tech expos.

The ICFAI Management school offers marketing strategy simulations, mock auctions, business ideation pitches, comprehensive problem-solving sessions, crisis management drills, general knowledge quizzes, and market dynamics challenges.

The ICFAI Law school hosts simulated environmental court proceedings, impromptu constitutional debates, sustainability showcases, environmental film screenings, mediation workshops, legal innovation sessions, and sociopolitical cartoon exhibitions.

The ICFAI Architecture school encourages artistic expression through personalized tote bag designs and character-themed performances.

The ICFAI Social Sciences school facilitates debates at the intersection of economics and psychology, argumentative skill-building sessions, photography showcases focusing on societal themes, and vibrant art festivals reflecting social narratives. This event promises to foster a sense of community and shared learning across various fields.
